About Craftholic

Craftholic is a series of huggable and stylish plush cushions designed by the Japanese designer, Ikuko Yamamoto. The uber soft printed cushions are perfect for your everyday as they look just as awesome strewn across the sofa as they feel squeezed between the arms!
